Strategic Planning

Develop Long-Term Organizational Vision and Execution Strategies

Strategic planning is a comprehensive process of defining an organization's direction, making decisions on allocating resources, and determining long-term goals and strategies.

Key Learning Objectives

  • Develop comprehensive organizational vision
  • Create actionable and measurable strategic goals
  • Analyze internal and external environmental factors
  • Design flexible and adaptive strategies
  • Implement effective performance monitoring mechanisms
