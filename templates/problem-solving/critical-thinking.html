Critical Thinking

Develop Analytical and Strategic Mental Frameworks

Critical thinking is an essential cognitive skill that involves objectively analyzing and evaluating information to form a judgment, challenge assumptions, and solve complex problems effectively.

Key Learning Objectives

  • Analyze information objectively and systematically
  • Question assumptions and recognize cognitive biases
  • Develop independent and rational thought processes
  • Evaluate arguments and evidence critically
  • Make well-reasoned and informed decisions
